Common Questions

Q: How do solar panels work?
A: Solar panels work by converting sunlight into electricity using photovoltaic cells.
Q: Are there any government incentives for solar installations?
A: Yes, there are various incentives and tax credits for solar installations, depending on your location.
Q: What is the lifespan of solar panels?
A: Solar panels can last for 25-30 years or more with proper maintenance.
